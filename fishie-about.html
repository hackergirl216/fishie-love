About my ♥ for Fishies

Welcome to my I Love Fishies website. On this site, I shall be declaring my love for fishies. They are beautiful and glorious. I love them so very much. Thinking about cute fishies have gotten me through a lot of hard times in my life. When I am feeling down, I google cute fish pictures. Check out cute pictures of fishies on the Gallery page. 10/10 would recommend. I think fishies are such a positive part of this meaningless existence. They really bring light to the darkness. I started this page in a very dark time of my life. This fishie website has brought some joy to my otherwise sad life experience.
